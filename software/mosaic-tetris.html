Mosaic Tetris

Mosaic Tetris was a game played from the terminal that I made as my first introduction to the Go programming language. It is similar to Tetris, except you can put the pieces anywhere on the board and they don't fall down. The catch: the pieces cannot be rotated.

You can download Mosaic Tetris from my main website or, if you're already on tilde.town, play it with the command 

town mosaic-tetris
.
